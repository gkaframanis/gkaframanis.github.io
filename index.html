Mini Bio

I was born in Thessaloniki and at a young age I moved to Lamia. I graduated from the 3rd high school of Lamia with the grade 19.3 / 20 but at that point I had no clue what I wanted to do with my life, so I ended up studying Civil Engineering in the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki. It didn't take much time to realize that this wasn't the path I was supposed to follow. I just couldn't see myself as a civil enginner for the rest of my life, but there still wasn't something else that I was feeling passionate about. It took me some time to discover my love for computers, internet, programming and computer science in general. That was what I wanted to study. So I gave the exams once again and I entered first in the departent of Computer Science and Biomedical Informatics of the University of Thessalia, from where I got my bachelor's degree with the grade 8.06 / 10.
